# Objective 4.1 - Theme 4: Monthly Rating and Review Trends

Use the **Monthly Rating and Review Trends** visual together with the year and category slicers to examine how **monthly review count** and **average rating** change within a selected year, in order to identify the periods in which the selected Nike categories receive the strongest customer evaluation.

- `Specific`: Monthly analysis of `review_count` and `average_rating` for the selected year and category context.
- `Measurable`: Uses monthly values of `review_count` and `average_rating` shown in the `Monthly Rating and Review Trends` visual.
- `Achievable`: The required review date, category, and rating fields are available in the prepared review dataset and current dashboard design.
- `Relevant`: Supports the analytical problem by showing when customer satisfaction signals are strongest within the observed review period.
- `Time-bound`: Evaluated within a selected year from the observed review period `2024` to `2026`, with month-level comparison inside that year.

## Suggested chart evidence
- Combo chart: Title = `Monthly Rating and Review Trends`, Axis = month, Values = `review_count` and `average_rating`, filtered by selected `year` and `category`.
- Optional supporting cards: `Total Reviews`, `Average Rating`, `Average Reviews`.
