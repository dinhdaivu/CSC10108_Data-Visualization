# Objective 3.1 — Product Influence Analysis

Identify the **top products and categories driving daily unit sales** from **February 20, 2026** to **March 20, 2026** using the current `Top products` visual and dashboard filters.

- `Specific`: Product-level and category-level contribution analysis focused on unit performance.
- `Measurable`: Uses `daily_units_sold` ranking, top-product share of total units sold, and category composition of top products.
- `Achievable`: Product name, category, and daily unit fields are available in the current dashboard model.
- `Relevant`: Directly supports the page purpose of understanding product influence on sales outcomes.
- `Time-bound`: Evaluated within the analysis window from `20-02-2026` to `20-03-2026`.

## Suggested chart evidence
- Horizontal bar chart: `Top products` by `Sum of daily_units_sold`.
- Legend split: category contribution among top-ranked products.
- KPI cards: `Sum of daily_units_sold`, `Distinct Products`.
