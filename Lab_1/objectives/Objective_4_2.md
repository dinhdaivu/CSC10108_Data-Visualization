# Objective 4.2 - Theme 4: Rating Comparison Across Years

Use the **bar chart of ratings by year** to compare customer evaluation levels across **2024**, **2025**, and **2026**, in order to determine whether Nike product ratings remain consistent or show meaningful changes over time.

- `Specific`: Year-based rating comparison using the average rating level for each year.
- `Measurable`: Uses differences in `rating` or `average_rating` across `2024`, `2025`, and `2026`.
- `Achievable`: The required rating and review-date fields are available in the prepared review dataset and dashboard visuals.
- `Relevant`: Supports the analytical problem by showing whether customer satisfaction changes over time and how review behavior may align with perceived product quality.
- `Time-bound`: Evaluated across the yearly periods `2024`, `2025`, and `2026`.

## Suggested chart evidence
- Bar chart: Axis = `year`, Values = `average_rating` or aggregated `rating`.
- Optional supporting visual: review count trend by year for context.
