# Objective 4.2 - Theme 4: Monthly Comment Trends for Top Products

Use the **Monthly Comment Trends for Top Products** line chart together with the year and category slicers to compare **monthly comment volume** among the most-discussed Nike products within a selected year, in order to determine which products consistently attract the most customer attention and whether review activity is concentrated in a small number of products.

- `Specific`: Product-level monthly comparison of comment volume for the most-commented products in the selected year.
- `Measurable`: Uses monthly `review_count` or comment count by `product_name` in the `Monthly Comment Trends for Top Products` visual.
- `Achievable`: The required product identifiers, review dates, and comment counts are available in the prepared review dataset and dashboard visuals.
- `Relevant`: Supports the analytical problem by showing which products generate the strongest customer discussion and how that attention is distributed across products.
- `Time-bound`: Evaluated within a selected year from the observed review period `2024` to `2026`, with month-level comparison inside that year.

## Suggested chart evidence
- Line chart: Title = `Monthly Comment Trends for Top Products`, Axis = month, Values = `review_count`, Legend = `product_name`, filtered by selected `year` and `category`.
- Optional supporting visual: `Monthly Rating and Review Trends` for comparison with rating performance.
